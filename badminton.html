Badminton E-Commerce

The Badminton e-commerce platform is an online store that specializes in selling badminton products to customers. The platform offers a variety of product categories, including racquets, shoes, shuttlecocks, and others.

To access the features and make purchases on the platform, customers need to register an account. During the registration process, customers provide their personal information, such as name, email address, and contact details. Once registered, customers can log in to their accounts to browse and purchase items.

When logged in, customers have the ability to add items to their shopping cart. They can select products from any of the available categories, including racquets, shoes, shuttlecocks, and others. The cart allows customers to review their selected items, update quantities, and remove items if desired.

After customers have finalized their selection, they proceed to the checkout process. During checkout, customers are prompted to choose their preferred payment method. The platform offers multiple payment options, including Touch and Go, Boost, Grab, and bank transfer. Customers can select the method that suits them best.

Once the payment is successfully processed, customers receive a receipt as confirmation of their purchase. The receipt contains details such as the order number, purchased items, payment method, billing and shipping information, and the total amount paid. Customers can keep the receipt for their records and reference.
