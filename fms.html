Financial Management System

The Faculty Management System being developed has the capability to perform analysis for each faculty and provide visual representations in the form of bar charts for staff members to gain an overview of relevant information.

Within the system, staff members can access functionalities that allow them to view statistic reports and perform analysis specific to their faculty. These reports are automatically generated by the system based on the available data. By leveraging the collected information, the system provides valuable insights and visual representations in the form of bar charts.

The bar charts serve as a graphical representation of data, presenting key metrics and trends in a visually appealing manner. Staff members can utilize these charts to gain a quick overview of important aspects such as spending patterns, income generation, and other relevant statistics specific to their faculty. This visual representation helps staff members to comprehend and interpret data more effectively, enabling them to make informed decisions and take necessary actions based on the insights derived from the analysis.

By incorporating analysis capabilities and visual representations through bar charts, the system enhances the staff's ability to understand and monitor the performance of their respective faculties. It empowers staff members with data-driven insights, facilitating effective management and decision-making within the academic institution.
